DCPU-16

An assembler, disassembler and emulator for the virtual DCPU-16 written in Python and C. The DCPU-16 is intended to form the basis of a future video game by Mojang called 0x10c.

DCPU-16 Highlights

  • 16 bit words
  • 0x10000 words of ram
  • 8 registers (A, B, C, X, Y, Z, I, J)
  • program counter (PC)
  • stack pointer (SP)
  • extra/excess (EX)
  • interrupt address (IA)

Implemented Specifications

  • DCPU-16 1.7
  • LEM1802
  • Generic Keyboard
  • Generic Clock
